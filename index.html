Our amazing projects

Quel Fior Di Gonna & Il Gran Cucito

It was July 2019 and we wanted give up with our frenetic city time so we decided to make some videos in the nature. We found this beauty place in Pontelagoscuro (Ferrara, Italy) and we build up 2 coreographies related to each other following the waves of peace of the nature. The exhibitions of these performances, with the crickets chirping sounds, bring all the peace of the nature with them, people in the exhibitions starts to feeling love for life and a new type of time conception that they maybe have never discovered.
